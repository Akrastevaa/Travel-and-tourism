"In Rome there is one of the most romantic and famous monuments of love - the Trevi Fountain. The interesting fact is that it is believed that if you toss a coin into the fountain, the backbone to the water, you will return to Rome again in the future. But if you throw two coins, it means you will fall in love with a Roman or a Roman. And, finally, if you throw three coins, it means you will marry this man. Millions of coins are collected from the fountain every year and they are used for charity."

Listed here you can find some of the destinations:

  1. Galapagos Islands, Ecuador

    2. These isolated islands are known for their wealth of unique species of animals and plants that are not found anywhere else in the world.


  2. La Paz, Bolivia

    3. Located high in the Andes, La Paz is the highest capital in the world. The city offers a unique combination of colonial architecture, beautiful panoramic views and a rich cultural scene.


  3. Hwang Hoa, Vietnam

    4. This small mountain town is located in northern Vietnam and offers magnificent landscapes, beautiful waterfalls and one of the largest color plantations in the world.


  4. Machu Picchu, Peru

    5. Known as the "lost city of the Incas", Machu Picchu offers incredible archeological ruins, impressive landscapes and cultural wealth.
